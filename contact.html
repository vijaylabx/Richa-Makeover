Common Inquiries

How far in advance should I book my bridal makeup?

add

We recommend booking at least 6 to 9 months in advance, especially for peak wedding seasons (May–September), to ensure your preferred date is available.

Do you provide on-site services for destination weddings?

add

Absolutely. Our team travels internationally for destination weddings. Travel and accommodation fees are tailored based on the location.

What brands of products do you use in the atelier?

add

We use exclusively premium, high-definition products from brands like Chanel, Dior, Pat McGrath, and Hourglass to ensure longevity and a camera-ready finish.
